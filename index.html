All About Gacha Life

Gacha Life is a role-playing game that allows users to create and customise anime-styled characters choosing from a choice of outfits to wear, and their appearance like their hair, eyes, etc. Users can choose and play from 8 mini-games, create skits in Studio Mode, create scenes, and chat with other players.
